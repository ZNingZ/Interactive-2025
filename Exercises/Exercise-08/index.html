Dark & Stormy

How to Make It

Ingredients:
- 2 oz dark rum
- 3 oz ginger beer
- 1/2 oz lime juice

Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in the rum, top with ginger beer, and finish with fresh lime juice. Garnish with a lime wedge.
